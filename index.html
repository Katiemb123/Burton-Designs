THE DESIGNER

KATIE BURTON

Based in Arkansas, Katie Burton brings a refined eye and deeply personal approach to every project — crafting spaces that are as functional as they are beautiful.

With a specialty in interior decorating and spatial styling, Katie works closely with each client to understand how they live, what they love, and how their home can better serve them — from the first consultation to the very last finishing touch.

Burton Designs offers complete design coordination, including full remodeling and interior work through trusted partners and in-house licensed construction services.
