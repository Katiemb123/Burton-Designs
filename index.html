ABOUT

KATIE BURTON

Based in Arkansas, Katie Burton brings a refined eye and personal touch to every project — creating spaces that are as functional as they are beautiful.

With a specialty in interior decorating and spatial styling, Katie works closely with each client to understand how they live, what they love, and how their space can better serve them.

Burton Designs offers full-service design coordination, from initial concept through final installation — including complete remodeling and interior work through trusted partners and in-house construction services.
